32  /**
33  * @brief computes configurational forces in KSDFT
34  *
35  * This class computes and stores the configurational forces corresponding to geometry optimization.
36  * It uses the formulation in the paper by Motamarri et.al. (https://link.aps.org/doi/10.1103/PhysRevB.97.165132)
37  * which provides an unified approach to atomic forces corresponding to internal atomic relaxation
38  * and cell stress corresponding to cell relaxation.
39  *
40  * @author Sambit Das
41  */
